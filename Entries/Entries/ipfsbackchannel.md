# ipfsbackchannel — Founder Entry  
*(Alternates: IPFS Back-Channel, Backchannel-IPFS)*

---

## Plain Definition  
**ipfsbackchannel** is a private or semi-private communication pathway built using IPFS (InterPlanetary File System) that enables individuals or communities to exchange information **outside the reach of centralized platforms.**

In simple terms:  
**ipfsbackchannel = a censorship-resistant messaging lane.**

---

## Technical Definition  
An IPFS backchannel leverages:

- distributed content addressing  
- decentralized file hosting  
- node-to-node replication  
- resilient gateway access  
- private CID sharing  

to create communication routes that cannot be:

- deleted  
- censored  
- shadow-banned  
- throttled  
- algorithmically filtered  
- removed by third parties

It is *not* inherently anonymous — but it is **resilient** and **independent**.

---

## Parable / Use Case  
Two researchers exchanging early drafts of a discovery do not trust email providers or social platforms to keep their documents intact.

Instead, they:

- write drafts  
- create CIDs  
- share the CIDs privately  
- update versions through IPFS  
- maintain a full history without middlemen  

They are communicating through an **ipfsbackchannel** — secure, quiet, unstoppable.
