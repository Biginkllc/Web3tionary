# 10. Governance Cycle (Simple v0.1)

1. Term proposed  
2. Community discussion (optional)  
3. DAO review  
4. DAO vote  
5. Entry merged / refined  
6. IPFS archival  

Founder may intervene if necessary to maintain coherence.

---

# 11. Versioning & Revisions

- All changes tracked by Git commits  
- Major updates receive version bumps (v0.2, v0.3, etc.)  
- Deprecated entries remain in archive for historical study  
- Concepts may evolve; raw forms never change

---

# 12. Tone, Ethics & Cultural Sensitivity

Web3tionary preserves:
- honesty  
- creativity  
- transparency  
- cultural awareness  
- historical truth  

It must not:
- erase nuance  
- sanitize culture  
- remove the puzzle  
- simplify complex lived experiences  

The dictionary reflects **the world as it is experienced**, not as institutions prefer it to be.
